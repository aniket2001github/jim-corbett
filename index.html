POPULAR TOURISM ZONES

Jim Corbett National Park

Dhela Zone

The Dhela Safari Zone spread more than 1173 hectares is the 6th ecotourism zone of Corbett Tiger hold that was kept on CTR the travel industry map in the year 2014.

Durgadevi Zone

Nestled in the northeastern corner of the Corbett National park, the Durgadevi zone gloats of having the most gorgeous scene in the Corbett National park.

Sitabani Zone

Sitabani is a hard backwoods region. Not a piece of the Corbett Tiger Reserve, however is the main spot for bird watching in Jim Corbett. The region is famous due to Ramayana.

Sonanandi Zone

Sonanadi untamed life offers a serene setting to partake in nature's magnificence and peacefulness with the sound of birds and a feeling of synchronization with natural life.

Jhirna Zone

Corbett National Park has six ecotourism zones, among them the Jhirna Zone, which is exceptionally well known. The Jhirna zone was added in 1994. furthermore, thought about perhaps of the best zone.
